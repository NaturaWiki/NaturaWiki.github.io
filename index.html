Objectives
NaturaWiki is an online encyclopedia that aims to provide personal research on natural psychoactive or physiological substances in a scientifically-grounded manner provided by the community and personal research. This is accomplished by:
• only providing information on substances scientifically proven to have psychoactive or physiological effects.
• documenting mechanisms of action, physical and cognitive effects; overall providing all important information about the substance.
• implementing research gathered from multiple published articles from expert and crowd-based sources.
